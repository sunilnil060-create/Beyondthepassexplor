A few simple, secure ways to pay for your Bhutan trip — choose whichever option is most convenient for you.

Home Travel Info Payment Process

We offer several payment options for your Bhutan trip booking. Indian nationals may pay in INR; all other foreign nationals are requested to pay in USD.

Have questions about payment? Message us any time — details below.

Traditional Wire / Bank-to-Bank Transfer

Send the payment directly from your bank, either in person at a branch or through your bank's online transfer service.

Many guests transfer funds straight from their bank's website. If your bank allows you to search for the receiving bank, check whether Bhutan National Bank appears in the list — if it does, you can send the transfer directly, without routing it through a correspondent bank.

If you're filling out a paper wire transfer form at your branch, you may see two SWIFT codes: one for a correspondent bank and one for the bank in Bhutan. The one that matters is Bhutan National Bank's SWIFT code, BNBTBTBT. If the form only has space for one bank, use Bhutan National Bank and its SWIFT code — the correspondent bank isn't needed.
