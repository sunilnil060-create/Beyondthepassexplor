Our Story

Born in the Valleys We Still Call Home

Beyond The Pass Exploration started with a simple idea: the best way to understand Bhutan is through the eyes of the people who grew up in its valleys. What began as a small team of local guides taking friends-of-friends up to Tiger's Nest has grown into a full-service tour operator — without ever losing that personal, unhurried way of showing people around.

Today we're a TCB-licensed operator (#1034899) built entirely around Bhutanese guides, drivers, and hosts who know these roads, dzongs, and festivals firsthand. Every itinerary we plan pays the Sustainable Development Fee in full and channels part of every booking back into the communities we pass through.

Ghana Shyam Chhetri Founder & Lead Guide
